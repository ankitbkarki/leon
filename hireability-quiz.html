Your Hireability Score is a comprehensive assessment of your job market readiness. It evaluates various factors that employers consider when making hiring decisions, including your skills, experience, education, and professional presence.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this Hireability Score?

Our quiz provides a general assessment based on common hiring criteria. While it offers valuable insights, actual hireability can vary based on specific job requirements and market conditions.

How can I improve my score?

The quiz provides personalized recommendations based on your answers. Focus on areas with lower scores, such as gaining more experience, obtaining relevant certifications, or enhancing your online presence.

How often should I retake this quiz?

We recommend retaking the quiz every 3-6 months or after significant career developments, such as completing a certification, gaining new experience, or changing jobs.
